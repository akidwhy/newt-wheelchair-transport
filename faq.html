Scheduling a ride is easy! You can call us directly at (630) 542-6398 — we're available 24 hours a day, 7 days a week. You can also fill out our online contact form and we'll get back to you promptly. We recommend booking in advance when possible, especially for medical appointments.
We recommend booking as early as possible — ideally 24 to 48 hours in advance for scheduled appointments. However, we understand that situations arise unexpectedly. Contact us and we'll do our best to accommodate your needs, even on short notice.
Yes! We operate 24 hours a day, 7 days a week — including weekends and major holidays. We believe that transportation needs don't take days off, and neither do we. Whether it's Christmas morning or a Sunday afternoon, we're here for you.
Absolutely. Many of our clients rely on us for regular weekly or bi-weekly appointments such as dialysis, physical therapy, or chemotherapy. We can set up a recurring schedule that works for you, giving you peace of mind and one less thing to worry about.
Our rates are competitive and transparent — no hidden fees, ever. Pricing varies based on distance and specific needs. Please call us at (630) 542-6398 for a free, no-obligation quote. We're committed to making our service accessible and affordable.
We accept all major credit cards (Visa, Mastercard, American Express, Discover), cash, and checks. Payment is typically collected at the time of service. If you have a special arrangement needed, just let us know when you book.
No! A caregiver or family member is always welcome to ride along at absolutely no additional charge. We understand how important it is to have support during transport, and we would never put a price on that.
We appreciate our loyal clients and are always willing to discuss arrangements for recurring trips. Please call us to talk about your specific situation — we'll do our best to find a solution that works for you.
We serve the greater Chicagoland area, including Chicago and surrounding suburbs such as Naperville, Aurora, Joliet, Elgin, Evanston, Schaumburg, Bolingbrook, Oak Park, Tinley Park, and many more. We also offer long-distance service — just call ahead to discuss your needs.
Yes, absolutely. We regularly pick up and drop off at nursing homes, skilled nursing facilities, assisted living communities, hospitals, and rehabilitation centers. We're experienced working with facility staff to ensure a smooth, coordinated pickup process.

Medical appointments are a big part of what we do, but we also love taking clients to weddings, birthday parties, church services, family gatherings, shopping trips, airport transfers, christenings, group events, and more. Life is meant to be lived fully — we're here to help make that possible.

Yes! While we primarily serve the Chicagoland area, we are happy to discuss long-distance trips. Whether you need transport to a hospital in another city or want to attend a special event out of the metro area, give us a call and we'll work with you to make it happen.
We use comfortable, modern, ADA-compliant ramp vans specifically designed for wheelchair transport. Our vehicles are spacious, climate-controlled, and regularly inspected for safety. We take pride in maintaining a clean, comfortable fleet.
100%. All of our vehicles fully meet Americans with Disabilities Act (ADA) standards. They are equipped with hydraulic or fold-out ramps, proper wheelchair tie-down systems, and appropriate seating configurations to ensure a safe and dignified ride for every passenger.
