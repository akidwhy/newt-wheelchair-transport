Who We Are

More Than Just a Ride

N.E.W.T. — Non-Emergency Wheelchair Transport was founded with one core mission: to give wheelchair-bound individuals the freedom to live their lives fully. Whether it's a doctor's appointment, a family gathering, or a night out with friends, we believe your mobility challenges should never limit your world.

As a family-owned and operated business, we bring a personal touch to every single trip. You won't reach a call center — you'll speak with someone who genuinely cares about you and your loved ones.

✓
Trained, compassionate drivers

Our team undergoes thorough training to ensure the safety and comfort of every rider.

✓
Stair transport specialists

We have the equipment and expertise to transport clients safely up or down stairs — a capability many services lack.

✓
Fully ADA-compliant ramp vans

Our modern fleet meets all ADA standards for comfortable, safe wheelchair transport.

Our Values

The Principles That Drive Us

Every decision we make — from the vehicles we drive to the way we answer the phone — is guided by a set of core values.

❤️

Compassion

We treat every rider with the warmth, patience, and respect they deserve. Our clients are never just a trip — they're people we care about.

🛡️

Safety

Safety is our top priority. Our drivers are trained, our vehicles are maintained, and our equipment is inspected before every ride.

⏰

Reliability

When you count on us, we show up — on time, every time. We know your schedule matters, and we take that responsibility seriously.

🤝

Dignity

We believe every person deserves to be treated with dignity. Our approach is always respectful, never rushed, and always attentive.

🏡

Community

Excellence
